Cheesecake

Description

This cheesecake recipe is the only one that you will ever need!

I took a screenshot of it, so sadly I don't know where the recipe is from. This is why I am here to explain!

Ingredients

  • butter (100g)
  • biscuits (200g)
  • cream cheese (600g)
  • low-fat quark (200g)
  • sugar (200g)
  • starch (3 tablespoons)
  • egg (1 large)
  • cream (150g)
  • lemon juice (2 tablespoons)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. For the bottom, melt the butter in a pan, pulverize the piscuits and mix them together. Spread on a 26cm round pan.
  3. For the filling, whip together the cream cheese, quark, sugar and starch until creamy.
  4. Carefully fold in the egg, cream and lemon juice.
  5. Spread evenly on the pan and bake in the oven for 45 minutes until the edge is golden brown.
