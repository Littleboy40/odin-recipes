Meme

Description

There really needs to be a recipe for creating memes, as some memes out there are booboo the fool.

Fear not, peasants, for I will come to thy rescue and teach thee how thee'hest can makest a good meme!

Ingredients

  • a funny image
  • a good idea for a joke

Steps

  1. Place the image in a text editor.
  2. Think of a good and funny font to use (I'd recommend Comic Sans).
  3. Place a text box with that font on the top of the image with the first half of your joke.
  4. Do the same thing for the second half of the joke on the bottom of the image.
